# Code of Conduct

We expect all contributors to follow a friendly, respectful, and inclusive code of conduct. This document outlines our expectations and explains how to report unacceptable behavior.

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in communications
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Avoid discriminatory, offensive, or harassing comments

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or hate speech of any kind
- Targeted insults or slurs
- Threats of violence or illegal activity

## Reporting an Incident

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening an issue or contacting the project maintainers directly in private.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove material or ban users for behavior that is not aligned with the Code of Conduct. Depending on the severity, this may mean a warning, temporary block, or permanent ban.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from common open-source project standards and is meant to be concise. If needed, you can update or expand it to match the Contributor Covenant or other official guidelines.
